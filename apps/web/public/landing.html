The problem

AI tools today are broken.

You're paying for chatbots that forget everything, can't work together, and send your data to someone else's cloud.

🧠

Amnesia by design

ChatGPT forgets everything every conversation

You explain the same context over and over. Your AI has no idea what you discussed yesterday, let alone last month. Every conversation starts from zero.

🤖

One bot, one trick

You can't have specialists collaborate

You want a writer, a coder, and a researcher working together on a project. Instead you get one generic assistant that's mediocre at everything.

🔒

Their cloud, their rules

Your data, your conversations, their servers

Every message, every document, every personal detail — stored on someone else's infrastructure. Model changes, price hikes, shutdowns? You have zero control.

📱

Disconnected from your life

AI lives in a browser tab, not in your workflow

Your AI can't send you a Telegram message, reply to Discord, or interact with the tools you actually use. It sits behind a chat window, waiting.

💸

Vendor lock-in

Switch providers = lose everything

Memory, preferences, agents — all tied to one provider. Want to use a different model? Start over. Want to export your data? Good luck.

🎛️

No real customization

"Custom instructions" is not customization

You want agents with specific roles, knowledge, and tools. What you get is a text box for "custom instructions" that barely affects behavior.

The solution

Everything AI should be.

👥
Multi-Agent Squads

A team, not a chatbot

Create specialized agents — writer, analyst, coder, researcher — and put them in squads. They collaborate using real protocols (ARCHER v2, NEXUS v3) with @mention routing, debate mode, and consensus building.

🧠
6-Layer Eidetic Memory

Total Recall™

Not just "remembers your name." Six layers: context window → session compaction → episodic recall → semantic knowledge → long-term facts → visual memory. Your agents get smarter with every conversation, forever.

🔍
Hybrid Search (FTS5 + Vector)

Find anything, instantly

Full-text search meets vector similarity, fused via Reciprocal Rank Fusion. Your agents find relevant context from thousands of conversations in milliseconds — keyword hits and semantic matches.

📡
Multi-Channel Reach

Meet users where they are

Bidirectional integration with Telegram, Discord, Slack, and webhooks. Inbound messages auto-route to the right agent, get a response, and reply — all in the background. Your AI isn't trapped in a tab.

🔄
Provider Freedom

Any model, any time

OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Ollama, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint. Quality-aware routing picks the best model for each task. Switch providers without losing a single memory.

🛡️
Security-Hardened

Battle-tested, not theoretical

Workspace sandboxing, path traversal protection, 25 blocked dangerous command patterns, circuit breakers, loop detection, encrypted credential vault. Audited at 8.8/10 by independent QA.
